QueerSafe

Combating negativity against the queer community.

A powerful Chrome extension designed to promote inclusivity and create a safer online environment for the queer community. This extension works tirelessly to detect and filter out offensive and derogatory language targeted at individuals within the queer community.

Enhance online safety and inclusivity with one click


Take a stand for online safety and inclusivity with a single click. Install our extension today and contribute to a more welcoming online environment for all.

Features

Instant Protection: Safeguard yourself and promote inclusivity online with a single click.

Real-time Content Filtering: Experience a worry-free browsing experience as our extension dynamically scans and filters offensive content in real-time.

Harmful words and slurs are intercepted and replaced, ensuring a safer and more respectful online space.
