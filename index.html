QueerSafe

Combating negativity against the queer community.

A powerful Chrome extension designed to promote inclusivity and create a safer online environment for the queer community. This extension works tirelessly to detect and filter out offensive and derogatory language targeted at individuals within the queer community.

Enhance online safety and inclusivity with one click
